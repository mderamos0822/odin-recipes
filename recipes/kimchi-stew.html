Kicmchi Stew Recipe

Description

A very much healthier Kimchi Stew recipe that's easier to digest.

Ingredients

  • 1 dried anchovy and kelp pack
  • 3 strips of meat of choice
  • 5 cups of water
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp of hot hot pepper paste
  • 1 tbsp of gochugaru (hot pepper flakes)
  • half tablesppon of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of water
  • 1-2 ounces of mung bean noodle
  • half cup of cabbage, cut
  • half ounce of enoki mushroom
  • half a medium onion
  • 1 Korean green pepper
  • half cup of fermented kimchi, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • half a pack of firm tofu, sliced
  • 12-16 sliced rice cakes
  • 2 green onions, cut into 1 inch pieces

Steps

  1. Pour 5 cups of water into a large pot
  2. Place dried anchovy and kelp soup pack into the pot and put on medium heat for 15 minutes
  3. Cut up meat into bite sized pieces in the pot for an extra 10 minutes
  4. Remove the pot from heat, strain, and set aside
  5. Combine garlic cloves, hot pepper paste, gochugaru, soy sauce, and water in a bowl
  6. Put the cabbage, onion, green onion, and pork at the bottom of the pot
  7. Add kimchi and seasoning paste
  8. Add pork belly from stock, rice cakes, tofu, and enoki mushrooms on the sides
  9. Add the mung bean noodles
  10. Add the stock until it covers 3/4 of the ingredients and place on medium high heat over a gas burner.
  11. Wait until ingredients cook and lower heat
