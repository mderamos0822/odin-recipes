Peanut Butter Porridge

Description

This porridge is affordable and so easy to make! It's high protein and vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cooked rice or 3 tbsp of uncooked rice
  • 3 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter
  • pinch o of salt
  • Salt & Pepper
  • ginger or apple ginger tea (optional)

Steps

  1. Add cooked rice to a pot with 4 cups of water on medium heat
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of peanut butter and stir until full dissolved (3-5 minutes)
  3. Keep heat on for 10-15 minutes with the lid on. If it boils over, crack the lid open and stir the pot.
  4. Add a punch of salt and stir
  5. Add in water and chicken broth
  6. Serve and enjoy, but don't eat the whole pot at once!
