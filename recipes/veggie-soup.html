Vegetable Soup Recipe

Description

This recipe will give you so much nutrition that you need for the day. Find yourself feeling energetic and looking glowy.

Ingredients

  • 1 L Carrot
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Half an Onion
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter
  • 1 tsp Avocado Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Garlic cloves or Garlic Powder
  • Pinch of Parsley and/or other herbs
  • 2 cups of water and 1 cup of chicken or beef broth
  • 1 lemon is optional

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat oil and butter in medium heat
  2. Chop carrots and Onions and add to the pot
  3. Chop Tomatoes in 4 and add to the pot after 5 minutes
  4. Add Garlic cloves and cook with lid for 10 minutes
  5. Add in water and chicken broth
  6. Cover with lid and let it cook for 10 minutes
  7. When it's boiling, add seasoning
  8. When all the veggies are soft, add it to a blender
  9. Pour in a bowl and add some lemon if you like!
